# Community & Forum SEO Reference

Guide for building brand visibility and earning citations through community platforms.
Read this file when working on off-page authority, brand awareness, or content strategy
that leverages Reddit, Quora, Facebook Groups, and other community platforms.

---

## Why Community SEO Matters in 2025–2026

Google's algorithms now prioritize authentic, peer-to-peer discussion over polished
corporate content. Reddit and Quora threads frequently rank on page 1 for queries like
"best [product] for [use case]", "is [service] worth it", and "[brand] review".

```
KEY FACTS:
  → Reddit threads appear in Google AI Overview citations regularly
  → "site:reddit.com" queries from users have grown 200%+ since 2024
  → Google treats community consensus as a strong E-E-A-T signal
  → AI engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity) scrape forums for "human-validated" information
  → Quora answers rank for long-tail, question-based queries across every niche

WHAT THIS MEANS FOR YOUR STRATEGY:
  → Community presence is no longer optional for competitive niches
  → Your brand must be part of conversations, not just search results
  → Forum participation builds the "experience" component of E-E-A-T
```

## Platform-by-Platform Strategy

### Reddit

The most frequently cited community platform in Google Search and AI Overviews.

```
FINDING RELEVANT COMMUNITIES:
  → Search: "site:reddit.com [your niche]" to find subreddits
  → For local businesses: r/[CityName], r/[StateAbbreviation]
  → For niche topics: search Reddit's native search for your primary keywords
  → Browse subreddit sidebars for related community links
  → Example for Houston tax services: r/houston, r/tax, r/personalfinance

ENGAGEMENT RULES:
  1. CREATE AN HONEST ACCOUNT PROFILE
     → Use a real name (or identifiable username), not a brand name
     → Complete profile bio with your expertise area
     → Never pretend to be a random user while promoting your business

  2. THE 90/10 RULE
     → 90% of your comments should be purely helpful — no brand mention
     → 10% can reference your business ONLY when directly relevant and helpful
     → Build karma and credibility before ever mentioning your brand

  3. BUILD CREDIBILITY FIRST (30–60 Days)
     → Comment on posts in your niche with genuine expertise
     → Answer questions with specific, actionable advice
     → Share experiences without linking to your site
     → Earn upvotes through helpfulness, not self-promotion

  4. CONTRIBUTE AS AN EXPERT
     → When you DO mention your brand, frame it as "I run a [business type] and..."
     → Share specific numbers, results, and real experiences
     → Accept and respond graciously to criticism or questions
     → Never use salesy language — Reddit communities detect and punish this instantly

WHAT TO AVOID:
  ❌ Dropping links to your site without adding value (shadow-ban risk)
  ❌ Multiple accounts promoting the same business (Reddit detects this)
  ❌ Copy-paste template responses across threads
  ❌ Automated posting or engagement tools
  ❌ Creating posts that are thinly-disguised ads
```

### Quora

Best for long-tail, question-based queries. Quora answers rank well on Google for
specific questions and are frequently cited by Perplexity and other AI engines.

```
STRATEGY:
  1. CREATE AN EXPERT PROFILE
     → Detailed bio with credentials, experience, and expertise areas
     → Profile photo (real person — builds trust)
     → Add "Credentials & Highlights" relevant to your niche

  2. FIND HIGH-IMPACT QUESTIONS
     → Search Quora for questions in your niche that have:
       - High follower count (many people want the answer)
       - Low-quality existing answers (your opportunity to provide a better one)
       - Recent activity (Google favors fresh Quora answers)
     → Focus on questions you can answer with unique expertise

  3. WRITE EXPERT ANSWERS
     → Lead with a direct answer (same as GEO content rules)
     → Include specific facts, numbers, credentials
     → 250–500 words per answer — substantive but focused
     → Include a natural mention of your experience (not your URL)
     → End with actionable advice the reader can use immediately

  4. LINK STRATEGY (CAUTIOUS)
     → Quora nofollow-tags most links — value is brand visibility, not link juice
     → Include ONE link to your site only when it genuinely helps the reader
     → Link to specific pages (not homepage), e.g., a relevant blog post or FAQ page
     → If the answer is strong enough, readers will search your brand name directly

CADENCE: Answer 2–3 questions per week consistently
```

### Facebook Groups

Critical for ethnic/linguistic community engagement, especially Vietnamese and
Hispanic communities in the US.

```
FOR VIETNAMESE COMMUNITY (HOUSTON EXAMPLE):
  → Groups: "Người Việt Houston", "Cộng Đồng Việt Nam Texas",
    "Thuế Mỹ - Người Việt", community-specific groups
  → Language: Post in Vietnamese (not English)
  → Content: Answer tax/insurance/immigration questions helpfully
  → Timing: Seasonal alignment — tax season (Jan–Apr), open enrollment (Nov–Dec)
  → Zalo groups: Parallel strategy — many Vietnamese communities use Zalo primarily

FOR SPANISH-SPEAKING COMMUNITY:
  → Groups: "Hispanos en Houston", "Latinos en Texas", niche-specific groups
  → Language: Post in Spanish
  → Same strategy: helpful, expert answers first, business mention second

ENGAGEMENT RULES:
  → Follow group rules strictly — many groups ban direct business promotion
  → Offer free value (answer questions, share knowledge) before any self-promotion
  → Use "I'm [name], I work in [field], and here's what I know..." framing
  → Share educational content, not ads
  → Build relationships with group admins (they can vouch for you)

CONTENT IDEAS FOR GROUPS:
  → "5 things you should know before filing taxes in the US" (educational)
  → "Common mistake I see every tax season" (experience-based)
  → Seasonal reminders with actionable tips (not promotional)
  → Answers to frequently asked questions in the group
```

## Content Repurposing: Forum → Website

Turn community insights into website content that ranks:

```
PROCESS:
  1. EXTRACT COMMUNITY QUESTIONS
     → Track the most-asked questions in your subreddits, Quora topics, and Groups
     → Note exact phrasing — this is how real people search
     → Each recurring question = potential FAQ or blog post

  2. CREATE WEBSITE CONTENT FROM FORUM INSIGHTS
     → Forum debate about "X vs Y" → comparison article on your site
     → Common question in 3+ threads → dedicated FAQ page with schema
     → Community-specific concerns → targeted content pages
     → Pain points people describe → "common mistakes" articles

  3. USE COMMUNITY LANGUAGE IN YOUR SEO
     → The words people use in forums = the words they type into Google
     → Update your keyword list with actual community phrasing
     → Example: Community says "khai thuế ở đâu?" not "dịch vụ khai thuế"
       → Target the phrase real people use

  4. ANSWER-FIRST CROSS-LINKING
     → When you answer a forum question well, write a more detailed version on your site
     → The forum answer = summary (with optional link for "full guide")
     → The website page = comprehensive answer with FAQ schema
     → Both versions can rank — the forum for Reddit-SERP, the page for organic
```

## Community SEO by Business Type

```
LOCAL SERVICE BUSINESS (tax, insurance, legal):
  Priority 1: Facebook Groups in target community language
  Priority 2: Reddit r/[CityName] and r/[industry]
  Priority 3: Quora questions about your service type
  Priority 4: Nextdoor for neighborhood-level visibility

BLOG / CONTENT SITE:
  Priority 1: Reddit subreddits in your niche
  Priority 2: Quora questions you can answer with expertise
  Priority 3: Industry-specific forums and Discord servers
  Priority 4: LinkedIn groups (for B2B)

E-COMMERCE:
  Priority 1: Reddit product/review subreddits
  Priority 2: Quora "best X for Y" questions
  Priority 3: Facebook Groups for niche hobbies/interests
  Priority 4: Product Hunt (for SaaS/tech products)
```

## Tracking Community SEO Results

```
METRICS TO MONITOR:
  ☐ Branded search volume — are more people searching your brand name?
  ☐ Referral traffic from Reddit, Quora, Facebook (GA4 → Acquisition → Source)
  ☐ Community mentions — are people mentioning your brand unprompted?
  ☐ Forum thread rankings — do threads featuring your brand rank on Google?
  ☐ AI citation from forum content — does AI cite forum discussions featuring you?

WHAT SUCCESS LOOKS LIKE:
  Month 1–2: Building credibility, no direct business impact expected
  Month 3–4: First organic brand mentions in communities
  Month 5–6: Referral traffic growing, branded search increasing
  Month 6+: Community members recommending your brand unprompted — this is the goal
```

## Red Flags — What Will Get You Banned or Hurt Your Brand

```
❌ Astroturfing — fake accounts posting positive reviews or recommendations
❌ Spam — posting the same link or message across multiple communities
❌ Vote manipulation — using multiple accounts to upvote your own content
❌ Ignoring negative feedback — not responding professionally to criticism
❌ Hard-selling — "Call us now for a free consultation!" in a community thread
❌ Posting AI-generated comments without editing — communities detect and punish this

✅ The only sustainable strategy: genuinely help people, consistently, over months.
   Brand visibility and citations follow naturally.
```
